Aisis

Aisis is a WordPress theme framework aimed at developers who want to develop rich, interactive, responsive child themes using a stable, well tested API.

Aisis provides developers and designers with various methods to override, implement and use to change either Aisis it’s self from the ground up or to build a child theme that is as unique as they are.

Aisis Strives to be as helpful as possible without getting in your way and hindering your creative process.

Aisis is not just another theme framework – its software that can be molded, shaped and designed to your liking!

Made for Developers

While Aisis provides people new to WordPress or WordPress themes the ability to change some core aspects of the core theme its self it is more made for developers who are looking to use a rich and stable API to extend Aisis or create rich and dynamic child themes.

Largely inspired by Zend Framework and OOP concepts Aisis does its best to adhere to the practices of OOP. It is also a well-documented and fast platform to develop on or against.

Creating Child Themes Has Never Been Easier

With Aisis our motto is to give you what you want, to give you the tools to create dynamic, rich, responsive and beautiful child themes without getting in your way. We want to provide you the tools you need as a designer or developer and then step out of your way and let you do whatever you want, whether it’s rebuilding the whole admin side or the building a whole child theme from the ground up.

We give you Hooks, Pluggable Functions, Templates, Modules, Loaders, Registers, Packages and much more that make creating and developing the easiest thing since breathing. And to boot we document everything cleanly, clearly and provide tons of examples, articles and explanations.

Aisis is Open Source

Aisis is not a premium theme framework; however we do support those who want to create premium components for Aisis. Aisis Is hosted here on Github and free of charge. That means you get all that rich, beautiful API, responsive design and much more for FREE!

Aisis is also hosted under the GPL3 meaning that you have complete freedom to do whatever you want!
