Aisis-Framework

Aisis is a free open source, 100% GPL compatible WordPress theme framework with more emphasis on framework then theme. While we manage to keep the theme framework easy to use for the average user Aisis it’s self is a revolution in theme framework development as we give the developers and designers the tools they need to build packages, child themes and plugins on top of our platform full of rich and well documented API’s.

What is Aisis?

Aisis is a highly sophisticated and highly developed theme famework, designed to be simple, easy to use and developer freiendly.

Aisis Come with a set of options that allows for easy configuration, simple set up and easy use. We pride our selves in creating the most simplistic, beautiful, responsive theme around. Even Thesis, a $120+ theme framework does not stand up against the awesome power of Aisis.

What Do we have to offer?

We have a clean, stable, useable product that allows developers to extend, enhance and build off of Aisis Theme. We also have a clean, easy to use options panel that allows for you to confidure your site the way you want it.

We have an API that is easy to use in child themes, plugins and more.

We do not build a product that stands alone, we build a set of products that stand together to make a platform which developers, designers and users can utilize to make amazing things that are both personal and professional.

In House Update!

We take care of our clients and users by giving them in house updates, this means when there is an update you are alerted right away! We do not hesitate to push out and fix. We are always looking for more ways to enhance, better and create a prouct that millions want to use!

Responsive Design

Aisis, From day one, is a responsive theme. That means it will look beautiful on any device, regardless of size. We have made it out mission to make sure the content you publish is accessible to all your clients and readers regardless of device.
